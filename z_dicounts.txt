Product based discount
----------------------
Direct Trade Discount 
	- flat discount on products
	- Eg: MRP maybe 100, but sale price 80
	
Promotional Discounts
	- discounts on promotional basis
	- Eg: Maybe a new product is given at lesser price for some time
	
Quantity Based Discounts
	- additional discount based on order quantity
	- offers when bulk orders taken

Category based discount
-----------------------
Seasonal Discounts
	- discounts on products for any special occasion
	- Eg: Offers on Christmas
